Welcome to Hard Rock Hotel, Pulau Pinang

The Hard Rock Hotel Penang is the first Hard Rock Hotel in Malaysia and is one of the many resorts and hotels that lie along the coastal stretch of Penang’s Batu Ferringhi. The Hard Rock brand is famous for its many hotels and cafes scattered all around the world and is well-known for its 80’s rock and roll vibe, themed hotel designs, and of course, the many fun-filled activities. So of course, it was hard not to get excited to return to Hard Rock Hotel Penang again, especially that moment when my car turned into the hotel and I spotted the signature purple sign and the huge guitar monument at the entrance of the hotel.

HOTEL

The Hard Rock Hotel Penang is well-known for its colorful, rock 'n' roll-themed rooms and upbeat vibe. The hotel offers a range of rooms and suites equipped with modern amenities, complemented by diverse dining options, including a buffet restaurant and beachside bar. Its standout facilities include a large free-form pool with water slides, a rock-themed spa offering massages and treatments, and regular live music events.
