PULAU TIOMAN

Guide: Everything You Need To Know

There are few things you need to know before going holiday in Pulau Tioman.

How To get To Pulau Tioman

Pulau Tioman is a 2 hour boat ride from Mersing Jetty on the south eastern coast of Malaysia. You can take a public boat for RM70 return and tickets can be bought directly from the office at the jetty.

Or getting there by

  • By Air: There are chartered flights from Kuala Lumpur and Singapore to Tioman Airport.

  • By Sea: Regular ferries operate from Mersing and Tanjung Gemok on the mainland. The ferry ride takes about 2 hours.

Where To Stay & Eat In Pulau Tioman

Four areas where people usually stay on Tioman.

  • Kampung Air Batang (also known as ABC)

  • Tekek Beach

  • Salang Beach

  • Juara Beach

Places to eat in Pulau Tioman

  • Tioman House Bungalows

  • Aqiss Bistro

What To Bring To Pulau Tioman[Tips]

  • Cash: ATMs are scarce, so carry sufficient cash.

  • Packing: Bring insect repellent, sunscreen, and waterproof bags.

  • Respect Nature: Follow eco-friendly practices and respect local wildlife.

Activities

  • Snorkeling and Diving

  • Hiking

  • Wildlife and Nature Tours

  • Water Sports
