Welcome to Silver Jubilee Park, Sungai Petani, Kedah

The administration of Silver Jubilee Park was managed by the Sungai Petani Municipal Council (MPSPK). The establishment is situated on the edge of the town centre, namely at Jalan Sungai Layar. The park was created to commemorate the Silver Jubilee of Sultan Abdul Halim Muadzam Shah, the present and 27th SUltan of Kedah.

Optimally, this place is for relaxing, having a picnic, and engaging in other activities. The residents of this area typically engage in a morning jog regularly. Additionally, there is the possibility of observing a group practising the art of Tai Chi in a wide outdoor area, where you are also welcome to participate.

More about Silver Jubilee Park

Silver Jubilee Park, also known as Sungai Petani, is a prominent example of a decent city due to its canopy-shaded park. It is the most valuable and frequently utilized gift that a city council or a candidate for public office can give to the people. In addition, this section functions as a recreational area for a conscious, tranquil afternoon break, a mini-zoo, and family and public activities. By participating in leisure activities and savouring a tranquil weekend, individuals can appreciate their exceptional quality of life. Additionally, a library is situated adjacent to the park, and the mosque of the Moroccan-influenced town is in view.


There are lakes with fish and tortoises. Individuals have the option to leisurely sit and appreciate the beautiful appearance of the park. Furthermore, there are cages built for housing eagles, owls, rabbits, and chickens, as well as several deer. The main activities in that location are running, cycling, sight-seeing, and aerobics. Additionally, a designated area with play equipment is available for the children's amusement. They can get enjoyment from engaging in activities with their friends.
